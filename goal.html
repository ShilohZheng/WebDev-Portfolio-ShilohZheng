Protecting Wildlife and Nature

I chose Goal 15: Life on Land because I care deeply about animals and nature. My love for games like Stardew Valley and Township shows how much I enjoy peaceful living, harmony with animals, and sustainable farming.

Stardew Valley encourages community and environmental care. Township lets players build a balanced life with animals, farming, and greenery. These games inspired me to support real-world actions that protect our planet.
