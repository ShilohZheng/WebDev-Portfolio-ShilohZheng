Animal Protection

I adore dogs and care deeply about animal welfare. I help manage a small TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) program in my area to humanely control the local stray cat population. By working with a few neighbors, we’ve fixed and vaccinated 12 cats over the past year, reducing fights and noise. It’s a small effort, but it aligns with UN Goal 15.5’s call to protect biodiversity—even at the local level.
