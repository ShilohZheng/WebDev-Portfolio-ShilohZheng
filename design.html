My Design Thinking Process

I followed the design thinking steps to build my site:

  1. Empathize: I imagined what users like my classmates would enjoy—simple, clean layouts with calming colors.
  2. Define: My goal was to showcase who I am in a fun and elegant way.
  3. Ideate: I drew inspiration from my favorite games and real websites I’ve visited.
  4. Prototype: I created sketches and wireframes before coding.
  5. Test: I asked friends to check if my site was clear and fun to explore.
